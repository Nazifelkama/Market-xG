# Glossary

## Market xG

A 0 to 100 probabilistic score describing the quality of the current market setup and the likelihood that conditions support continuation.

## Market Quality

The overall health of the market environment based on trend, participation, fundamentals, macro conditions, liquidity, sentiment, volume, and valuation.

## Opportunity Score

A score describing how attractive the current market setup appears relative to risk and potential continuation.

## Category Score

A 0 to 100 score for one market quality category, such as trend, breadth, macro, or sentiment.

## Confidence Score

A measure of how much trust should be placed in the current Market xG score based on data completeness, indicator agreement, and input quality.

## Market State

A human-readable classification of current conditions, such as healthy, improving, fragile, weakening, or stressed.

## Regime

A broad market environment with distinct behavior patterns, such as bull market, bear market, high-volatility market, or liquidity-driven market.

## Forward Return

The return measured after a defined future period, such as 1 month, 3 months, 6 months, or 12 months.

## Drawdown

The decline from a prior peak to a later low over a given period.

## Backtest

A historical validation process that applies the scoring method to past data and compares scores with later market outcomes.

## Lookahead Bias

An error where calculations accidentally use information that would not have been available at the time of the historical score.

## Data Completeness

The degree to which required input data is present, valid, and usable for scoring.

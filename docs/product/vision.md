# Market xG Vision

## Purpose

Market xG is a probabilistic market quality and continuation engine inspired by football expected goals (xG). In football, xG does not guarantee whether a shot will become a goal. It estimates the quality of the chance based on context.

Market xG applies the same idea to markets. It does not predict exact prices. It evaluates the current quality of the market setup and helps investors understand whether conditions appear healthy, fragile, improving, or weakening.

## Decision Support, Not Prediction

Market xG is designed as decision support. It helps answer questions such as:

- Is the market currently playing well?
- Is this rally healthy or fragile?
- Is money accumulating or distributing?
- Is continuation probability improving or weakening?
- Is the risk/reward profile attractive?

The system should support better reasoning, clearer risk awareness, and more disciplined review of market conditions.

## Main Outputs

- Market xG Score from 0 to 100.
- Category Scores from 0 to 100.
- Confidence Score.
- Market State.
- Human-readable explanation of the score and its drivers.
- Historical backtest results.

## Non-Goals

- No exact price prediction.
- No guaranteed buy or sell signals.
- No claims of certainty.

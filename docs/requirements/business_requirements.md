# Business Requirements

BR-001: The system shall calculate a Market xG score between 0 and 100.

BR-002: The system shall explain the drivers of the score in human-readable language.

BR-003: The system shall support historical validation through backtesting.

BR-004: The system shall avoid deterministic claims about future prices.

BR-005: The system shall support S&P 500 first, before adding VUSA.
